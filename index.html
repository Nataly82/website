Networks: Mobile Business

The idea of a network allows other devices to communicate important things. Also it will reduce the cost of connecting and particular networks will achieve these goals. Today's business networks include a combination of local area networks, wide area networks, and metropolitan area networks. All these networks are categorized based on geographic spans.

A local area network connects a group of computers in close proximity such as in an office building, school, or home. LAN's allow sharing of files, printers, games, and other resources. WAN's are essential for carrying out the day to day activities of many companies and government organizations. A wide area network spans a large geographic area such as a state, province, or country. A metropolitan area network is a large computer network, usually spanning a city. Most colleges, universites, and large companies that span a campus use an infrasturcture support by a MAN.
